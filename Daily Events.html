Daily Events

Ultimate Trial

  • Battle your way up the floors. It saves last progress and keeps you refreshed every day.
  • You can get characters by just partcipating such as Mature, Kensou and Chin.

Event Stage

  • Gold Rush = destroy a vehicle and exchange car parts to store for gold.
  • Female fighters = need a team of all females to fight. Gain food.
  • Mirage challenge = team event where you can get character fragments if you have that character.
  • Exp Rush = destroy a vending machine to gain soda to level up your characters.

Clone battle

  • You can only play against the clone that you have in your inventory.
  • Another way to get character fragments.
  • Opens around 1100 CST.

Fighter biography

  • You can only use the fighter once throughout the event.
  • You gain character fragments, gold, and esterics.

KOF tournament and Club battle

  • Sign up and get prizes. It does not matter how good or bad your team is.
  • Prizes given at 2000 hours CST

Bar

  • A great way to recruit characters to your roster.
  • You can spend 2000 diamonds to get a random fighter [aptitude 14 or lower] or 10 diamond tickets.
  • You can get a random fighter every 10 days of visiting the bar.
