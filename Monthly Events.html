Lucky Draw

  • Consist of 2 boss characters and 3 Aptitude13 characters.
  • Buy 5 costs 2200 diamonds.
  • No guarantee of gaining a character of choice.

Limited Chest

  • An auction for a boss. Each chest gives a user 10 points. At end of event, the top 30 with most points get prizes.
  • You have a small chance of getting the boss in a 3* version when you open chests in this event.

One Arm Bandit

  • Best and fastest way to gain diamonds. Save your diamonds for this event.
  • Customers who recharge a certain amount can get max benefit. FTP players get half benefit.

Scratch off

  • 3 free tries.
  • Get gold fast and free.
